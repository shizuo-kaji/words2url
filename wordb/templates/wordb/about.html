What is Words2URL

Words2URL is a simple online service that associates your favourite Word (or phrase) to Data such as URL, text, and short HTML.

Type "google" and you are taken to the famous search engine, as expected.
Type "penguin" if you like the birds.

Our service is meant to be used, for example, as a shared/personal bookmark, a pointer to your product page, an online business card, and an online meeting URL opener.

You do not have to memorise a complicated URL but just keep your favourite Word.
You do not have to text a long meeting URL but just tell your Word in person or over the phone.
Your tweet looks nicer with Word than with a cryptic URL of the fun video.

It is similar to the QR code but requires no camera or paper. All you need is your voice to tell Word.

How to use

Just type or speak your Word at Home, and you will be taken to the information associated to the Word.

Word-Data pairs can be registered at Add page on a first-come-first-served basis. Each Word is valid only for a certain period. The period can be extended at Edit page using Editkey chosen at the creation time. The associated Data can also be modified in the same manner.

Note that symbols and double spaces in Word are replaced by a single space.

Further usage

The service returns pure text for a GET request like '?query=Words' so that it can be used as a certain API. For example, you can store XML and retrive from your service. We hope to see interesting applications.
